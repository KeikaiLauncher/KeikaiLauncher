A fast, minimalistic and lightweight search-based launcher.

Use it in conjunction with your current launcher as an alternative app drawer to quickly launch your apps. You can also launch the app enabling its permanent notification or by long pressing/swiping up the home button.

It is an active fork of https://f-droid.org/packages/com.hayaisoftware.launcher which brings many fixes and improvements:<ul>
<li>Vector images</li>
<li>Screen orientation change and better scaling on larger devices</li>
<li>Application management from the home screen</li>
<li>Support for Android's UsageStats subsystem (API 21+)</li>
<li>and more</li></ul>
